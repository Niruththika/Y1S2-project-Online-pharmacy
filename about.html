Cancellation Policy


Online pharmacies often provide a set period of time within which you can cancel your order. This window usually spans a few hours to a few days after the order is placed. To qualify for a refund, you must cancel within this time frame.

Refund Eligibility: In general, you are entitled to a refund if you cancel your order within the allotted cancellation period. However, some online pharmacies could take a processing or cancellation fee from the amount of the refund.
